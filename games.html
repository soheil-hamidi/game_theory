Nim is played starting with rows of candies!

Chomp


Chomp is played starting with chocolate bar!
Players take turns chomping a square out of the chocolate bar along with any squares to the right and above.
Whoever takes the lower left square loses!

Take Away


Take Away is played starting with coins!
Players take turns taking one, two or three silver coins at a time.
Whoever takes the last gold coin wins!
